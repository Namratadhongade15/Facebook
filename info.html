Facebook Information Center

Welcome to the Facebook Information Center

Here you can find information about Facebook's policies, community standards, and privacy controls. We want to make sure that you have the information you need to make informed decisions about how you use Facebook and how you share your information with others.

Policies

Our policies help to create a safe and positive environment for everyone who uses Facebook. They explain what is and isn't allowed on the platform, and what happens when policies are violated.

Community Standards

Our community standards set out the rules for what is and isn't allowed on Facebook. They are designed to create a safe and positive environment for everyone who uses the platform.

Privacy Controls

We want you to have control over your information on Facebook. That's why we provide a range of privacy controls that allow you to decide who can see your information and how it is used.
